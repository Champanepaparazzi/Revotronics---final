About Us

About REC

Rajalakshmi Engineering College is an autonomous Institution affiliated with Anna University, which was established in the year 1997. As a proud Institution with 26 years of academic excellence it has been ranked as the third most preferred private Engineering college in Tamil Nadu. This Institution has been consistently providing high quality professionals by offering 19 UG, 11 PG courses, and Management studies along with a school of architecture and a school of nursing.

About ECE

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering was established in 1997. This department offers cutting-edge programs at the Undergraduate, Postgraduate, and Ph.D. levels. From its inception, the Department of ECE has fostered a culture of research orientation among its students, nurturing the development of highly-skilled researchers. More than 95% of eligible ECE students are placed in reputable companies each year. This department has consistently produced globally competent Electronics and Communication Engineers since its establishment.
